Welcome to the Flashcards app!
You're in the right place if you are looking to make flashcards to help you learn something new but don't want to use paper.

How to use:
If you want to type out both the front and back of the cards yourself, click on the first box on the screen to get started. You will be prompted to type content for each of the cards and in the lower left corner there will be a count keeping track of how many cards you have made and a button to click to actually use them.

Don't want to come up with all the content yourself?

So far, there are two other options for cards where you only have to come up with the front of the card and the information on the back will be returned to you!

For the Wikipedia option, if your topic of choice has a wikipedia page, the information returned will be the first paragraph and you just have to enter the page name. If you are interested in movie sentiment analysis, you have to do a little more work where you enter the name of a movie as well as some lines from it and the analysis of the lines will be displayed on the back of the card created.

In the future, there may be more special topic cards incorporated but the basic functionality of prompts and displaying a card count and display button will not change and there will always be the option of creating a set of cards with just one or all types of cards!
